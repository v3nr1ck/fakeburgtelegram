# Champaign County, Ohio — local authenticity bible

**Purpose:** Real place names, roads, schools, and businesses for Fake Burg Telegram satire.  
**Rule:** Use these for *setting* (where someone parks, shops, drives). Do **not** invent scandals about real living private people. Invented characters + real geography = the Onion local formula.

**Last researched:** July 2026 (public directories, village/city sites, visitor bureau, school sites). Businesses open/close — re-check before treating something as still open if it matters.

**Caution:** There is a *Mechanicsburg, PA* and a *Lewisburg, OH* (Preble Co.). We mean **Mechanicsburg, Champaign Co.** and **North Lewisburg, Champaign Co.**

## Business-name usage policy (for satire)

**Safe / good:**
- Character stops *at* a real place for coffee, pizza, candy, gas  
- “Line at Crabill’s,” “parking by the Gloria,” “after the fair on Park Ave”  
- Invented **characters** complaining about real **geography**

**Risky / avoid:**
- Claiming a real business did something illegal, bigoted, or scandalous  
- Fake quotes attributed to real named owners  
- Closing a real place “for health violations” you invented  

**When in doubt:** use real streets + invented businesses, or real businesses only as passive scenery.

## Quick “authenticity checklist” before publishing a draft

1. Town + school mascot match?  
2. Street exists in that town (Main is OK if town is named)?  
3. Highway direction roughly right (36 E–W, 68 N–S)?  
4. Business used only as scenery unless you’re sure?  
5. Not accidentally PA Mechanicsburg or Preble Co. Lewisburg?
