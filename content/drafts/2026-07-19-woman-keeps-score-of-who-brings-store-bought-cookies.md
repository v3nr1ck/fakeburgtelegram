**ST. PARIS** — Sources close to the **Graham Elementary** volunteer scene on **W. US Highway 36** confirm that at least one parent maintains a detailed internal ranking of which families “actually bake” versus which families “swing by **St. Paris Shoppers** with a plastic tray and confidence.”

“I’m not judging,” said the parent, while judging in the **Falcons** parking lot. “I’m noticing.”

Defenders of store-bought argued that the children eat three cookies and forget the geopolitical implications. The counterargument was a long silence and a smile that did not reach the eyes — same energy as the **Thrifty Falcon** clearance rack on Springfield Street.
