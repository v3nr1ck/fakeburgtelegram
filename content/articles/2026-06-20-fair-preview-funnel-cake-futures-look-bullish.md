**URBANA** — Organizers say this year’s **Champaign County Fair** at the fairgrounds on **Park Avenue** will feature classic midway energy, 4-H excellence, and enough powdered sugar to alter local weather patterns.

New this year: slightly clearer restroom signage and the same argument about whether elephant ears count as a meal — preferably settled after a stop at **Crabill’s** on the way home.

The Telegram will publish daily fair notes once the gates open. Bring cash. Bring patience. Bring someone willing to hold your lemon shake-up while you lose at the milk-bottle toss and circle **Park Ave** looking for a spot that isn’t mud.
