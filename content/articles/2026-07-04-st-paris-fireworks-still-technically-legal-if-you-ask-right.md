**ST. PARIS** — Community celebrations across western Champaign County went off with the usual mix of civic pride and questionable aerial shells launched from pickup beds near **W. Main** and side streets off **Springfield Street**.

Organizers of the St. Paris Homecoming picnic said the official show “remained family-friendly,” while unofficial shows “remained creative.”

“We love freedom,” said one resident who declined to give a last name but was standing next to a crate labeled *mortar* within earshot of **US-36**. “Also we love not getting a citation.”

Village officials asked residents to clean up cardboard tubes before trash day and to remember that “quiet hours still exist even if the calendar is red, white, and blue” — especially if your neighbor works early at **Graham** on the highway.
