**ST. PARIS** — Community celebrations across southern Champaign County went off with the usual mix of civic pride and questionable aerial shells launched from pickup beds.

Organizers of the St. Paris Homecoming picnic said the official show “remained family-friendly,” while unofficial shows “remained creative.”

“We love freedom,” said one resident who declined to give a last name but was standing next to a crate labeled *mortar*. “Also we love not getting a citation.”

Village officials asked residents to clean up cardboard tubes before trash day and to remember that “quiet hours still exist even if the calendar is red, white, and blue.”
