**MECHANICSBURG** — Graduates crossed the stage to applause that started early and ended late, which is how small towns say congratulations.

Speeches hit the usual notes — future, gratitude, don’t forget where you came from — and for once nobody checked their phone when the band played.

The Telegram congratulates the Class of 2026. Please drive carefully on the way to the open houses.
