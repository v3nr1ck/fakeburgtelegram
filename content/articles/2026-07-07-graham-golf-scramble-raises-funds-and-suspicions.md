**ST. PARIS** — The annual benefit scramble drew full carts and partial honesty as teams raised money for Graham athletics.

Organizers thanked sponsors and asked that any mulligans be “financially supported next year.”

Closest-to-the-pin went to a player who insisted the wind helped; longest drive went to a guy whose ball was found nearer the road than the fairway, which the rules committee called “a conversation.”
