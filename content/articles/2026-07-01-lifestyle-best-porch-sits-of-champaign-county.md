A good porch sit requires three things: a chair that has opinions, a beverage, and at least one vehicle to wave at.

**Mechanicsburg Main Street** scores high for foot traffic and low for privacy.  
**Township roads** score high for sunsets and medium for bugs.  
**Anywhere with a ceiling fan** is automatically in the top tier.
