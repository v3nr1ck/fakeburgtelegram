A good porch sit requires three things: a chair that has opinions, a beverage, and at least one vehicle to wave at.

**Mechanicsburg Main Street** scores high for foot traffic (Simple Coffee cups, Frosty’s smell) and low for privacy.  
**St. Paris Springfield Street** scores high for boutique browsing between sits.  
**Monument Square, Urbana** scores high for people-watching and medium for exhaust.  
**Township roads toward Cable / Rosewood** score high for sunsets and medium for bugs.  
**Anywhere with a ceiling fan** is automatically in the top tier.
