**CHAMPAIGN COUNTY** — Construction on Willow Dale Road continues for “approximately eight weeks,” a phrase that has already outlived several calendars in local kitchens.

Detour signs point confidently in directions that eventually loop back to the same flagger.

“I’ve seen my own house three times today,” said a commuter. “We’re bonding.”

Updates will be posted whenever the orange barrels move, which is roughly never and also constantly.
