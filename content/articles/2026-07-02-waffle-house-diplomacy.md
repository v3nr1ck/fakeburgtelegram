If Champaign County’s loudest arguments were relocated to a booth under fluorescent lights with hash browns on the table, half of them would end in syrup-based compromise.

Our proposal is simple: no public hearing starts until all parties have ordered. You may not amend a motion until the coffee has been refilled. Name-calling is redirected to the jukebox.

Local government is hard. Late-night waffles are easy. The Telegram endorses both — especially the waffles.
